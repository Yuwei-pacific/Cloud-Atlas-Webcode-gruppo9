EVERYTHING IS CONNECTED

  • DIRECTED BY LILLY WACHOWSKI TOM TYKWER ANDY WACHOWSKI
  • The story is a time-shifting weave of six interlinking narratives set in different eras and places. A time journey in which past, present and future merge: from the remote South Pacific of the nineteenth century to a post-apocalyptic future.Action, romance and destiny intertwine dramatically through the story showing how human connections overcome the barriers of time and space and where a single gesture can have an impact that spreads through the centuries.
  • The project aims to represent and to “give life” to the imaginary thread that connects the six stories, making it the key and recurring element throughout the website.A thread that will create three different representations to explain the three crucial aspects of the film.
